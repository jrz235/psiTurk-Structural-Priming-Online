(1) For the first task, you will see two images. You will then listen to an audio recording describing an event. Your job is to select which of the two (2) images the recording described. During the experiment, you may only listen to each audio recording once.

To make your selection, type the LEFT ARROW KEY (←) if you think that the image on the LEFT is a match and the RIGHT ARROW KEY (→) if you think that the image on the RIGHT is a match. You will automatically advance to the next task.

To record your response, click the "Record" button and speak into your microphone. Then click "Stop" when you are finished. During the experiment, you may only make one recording per question. On these questions, you must type "N" to advance to the next task.
